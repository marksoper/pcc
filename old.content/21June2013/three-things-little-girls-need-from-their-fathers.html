Dads Talk Sexuality With Daughters

Psychoanalyst and author of "Your Daughter's Bedroom" Joyce McFadden advises fathers on overcoming their fears so they can help their daughters be confident and healthy about sex later in life. Would you believe that preschool girls are more likely to have been taught the word "penis" than any word for their own genitalia?
